Engineer your ideas to life


The Undergraduate Student-initiated Experiential Learning (USEL) Program allows students to initiate a project of their interest under the guidance of a faculty member. What's more, you get complete access to the USEL Lab for all the tools you need! So what are you waiting for? Come, engineer your ideas to life!

Experiential Learning Courses


The Center for Engineering Education Innovation provides courses where you can experience engineering concepts, while using tools to make a final product. Using a flipped classroom approach, where the emphasis is on learning rather than teaching, these courses are fun and experiential. They are one of the best ways to cultivate the mindset that will eventually help you on your project.
